Del Mar Preschool

A Licensed California Child Care and Learning Arts Center

Classes are now open to all children ages 2-5 in San Diego County!
The Del Mar Preschool provides everything your child needs to thrive when they start Kindergarten.
Our staff are UCSD graduates, certified in behavioral health, nutrition, and human development.
In addition to our Preschool Program we provide Complete Childcare Services (overnight, weekend, and on-site care by appointment).

Classes We Offer

Here is more detailed information about the courses and specialties our preschool program offers:

Arts and Crafts

Children learn fundamental art skills and create art using different types of media including: paints, inks, markers, and chalk.

Language

In addition to prper English, children are immersed into secondary languages such as Spanish, Italien, and French.

Math

Children gain the fundamental concepts of math such as adding, counting, and geometry.

Physical Activity

Fitness is incopororated into our daily routine. Outdoor team based activities encourage healthy learning.

Reading and Story Time

Children are encouraged to read, and participate in group activities to understand story concepts.

Science and Technology

Children participate in experiments that help them learn about basic scinece concepts. Hands on learning with technology.
